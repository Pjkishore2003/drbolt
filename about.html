About Us

Dr Bolt is a web-based platform that connects vehicle owners with certified mechanics to book repair and maintenance services. With this platform, customers can easily book appointments with mechanics in their area, or even remotely if they're outside of the service area. The platform features a user-friendly interface that allows users to search for mechanics based on their location, availability, and specialty. Customers can view mechanic profiles, read reviews from previous customers, and book appointments at their convenience.

The platform also includes a scheduling and payment system that streamlines the booking process. Users can book an appointment and make payment online, making the entire process quick and convenient.Dr Bolt is built using responsive design principles, ensuring that the platform works seamlessly across different devices and screen sizes. This feature allows users to access the platform on any device, making it easy to book appointments on-the-go..
